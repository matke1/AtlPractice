What is Atlis?

Atlis is the community for local recommendations, where immediate, authentic referrals can earn you cash rewards.

The Atlis mission

We are here to make word of mouth count. When you really need a recommendation you can trust—anything from the perfect place for a first date to the most delicious pastrami sandwich—you don’t want to waste your time sorting through hundreds o f badly written and misleading reviews. You want to ask for advice and get answers right away from people who really know.

How does it actually work?

Atlis gives cash rewards to our users for every successful referral. Simply ask for a recommendation on the app, let the Atlis community share their favorites, and then visit the spot you like best. Then Atlis pays the user who referred you. Discovering what you love has never been so rewarding.

We are launching in New York, the city of adventure seekers

Where better than New York? In this city, every opportunity for food, fitness or entertainment is an adventure. New Yorkers are always seeking the best the city has to offer, which makes word of mouth really count.

So go ahead… discover, share, get rewarded.
